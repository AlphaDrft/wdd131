About Nueva Vizcaya

Nueva Vizcaya is a province rich in natural beauty and vibrant indigenous culture. From its majestic waterfalls and scenic mountain trails to its warm and welcoming people, it's a destination that invites exploration and connection with nature.

Top Destinations

Discover the must-see spots of Nueva Vizcaya. From serene waterfalls to impressive dams, each destination offers a unique experience.

Imugan Falls

A breathtaking waterfall hidden in the lush mountains of Nueva Vizcaya.

Capisaan Cave System

One of the Philippines' longest cave systems, perfect for explorers.

Magat Dam

One of the Southeast Asia's largest dams, offering breathtaking views and a peaceful atmosphere.

Food & Culture

Experience the warmth of Vizcayan hospitality through its local cuisine and colorful traditions. From freshly grilled tupig to the rhythmic dances of indigenous festivals, every experience tells a story.

Pancit Cabagan

A flavorful noodle dish popular across Northern Luzon, often enjoyed with boiled egg and crispy toppings

Tupig

Sweet, smoky rice cakes wrapped in banana leaves - a favorite local delicacy in Nueva Vizcaya.

Ipon Fish

A seasonal delicacy found in the rivers of the province, known for its unique flavor and texture.

Pack Light but Smart

Bring breathable clothing, water bottles, and hiking shoes for Nueva Vizcaya's outdoor adventures.

Check the Weather

Best time to visit is during the dry season (November-May when hiking trails and waterfalls are most accessible.
