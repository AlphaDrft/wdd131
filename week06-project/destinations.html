Discover Nueva Vizcaya's Natural Wonders

From majestic waterfalls to tranquil mountains - explore nature's finest treasures in the heart of Northern Luzon.

Nature and Waterfalls

Imugan Falls

A peaceful waterfall surrounded by lush greenery - perfect for hiking, swimming, and nature photography.

Bansing Falls

A popular summer destination located in Barangay Bansin, Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya - known for its scenic beauty, the falls offer a refreshing natural escape for residents and tourists.

Capisaan Cave

A stunning cave system ideal for explorers looking to experience the natural wonders beneath Vizcaya's hills.

Mountains & Adventure

Mount Pulag

A favorite for trekkers seeking adventure - famous for its "sea of clouds" and its exceptional view of the Milky Way Galaxy at dawn.

Magat Dam

One of Southeast Asia's largest dams, offering scenic views, calm waters, and a serene picnic atmosphere.

Kayapa Trail

Known for its breathtaking landscapes and challenging hikes, this is a must-visit for seasoned adventurers.
